New grads and career transitioners: feeling
lost in space?

They say you can’t get a job without experience, and you can’t get experience without a job.

You need 0 experience to blast off with Tech Fleet

We offer real-world learning opportunities whether you have experience or not. You can learn the ropes of product life while getting on-the-job training and mentorship from leads. And it’s free to you!

Choose your path to the Real World

Learn on the job and build experience while working with real clients. Learn the ways of UX and Product and Development from industry vets. Join a global community of aspiring Techies supporting each other’s growth.

Helping people learn by doing since 2020

Only jobs teach you some of the hard and soft skills you need to succeed in UX, Product, or Development. We teach them in Tech Fleet.

Let the Fleet work for you

Whether you’re a company or startup, for-profit or nonprofit, we offer services to help you accomplish your business goals. We offer pro bono services for nonprofits. We’ll work for tokens or equity. By being our client you’re helping aid in our mission to provide equal access to Tech.

We can help you

  • launch a new idea from conception
  • find your product-market fit
  • develip a web or mobile product
  • re-envision an existing product or service
  • Test new business ideas with users
  • Increase engagement with your user base
  • Curious whether we offer a service you don’t see? Let’s talk!
